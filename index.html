📖 How GoaLMint Works

🏠 Dashboard: View today's habits, streaks, XP, coins, and tokens. Switch between Morning/Evening routines.

➕ Adding Habits: Tap "+" to add a new habit with category, schedule, reminders, and more.

✅ Completing Habits: Tap "Done" on any habit. You earn +1 Mint Coin, +10 XP, and your streak increases. After the day passes, only Streak Tokens can save missed days.

👤 Avatar System: Your avatar grows with your habits! Health → Strength, Learning → Knowledge, Productivity → Focus, Creativity → Creativity. Level up, unlock new stages (Beginner to Legend), and buy cosmetics with Mint Coins.

🛒 Mint Shop: Spend Mint Coins on 30 premium themes, XP packs, and avatar cosmetics. Earn coins by completing habits.

🛡️ Streak Insurance: Earn Streak Tokens every 7-day streak. Use 1 token to save a missed day.

⚔️ Boss Battles: Start a challenge. Each daily completion damages the boss. Missed days heal it. Defeat bosses for rewards!

🧬 Habit DNA: See your habit breakdown by category.

✉️ Future Messages: Write messages that unlock after conditions (30 days, 100-day streak, etc.).

📖 Life Chapters: Organize habits into life phases.

🌍 World Impact: Choose a cause and see your simulated positive impact.

🏆 Achievements & Leaderboard: Earn badges and compete for monthly coin rewards.

🔗 Referral System: Share your unique code and both you and your friend earn 20 Mint Coins.

🔔 Notifications: Set reminder times for habits.

🎨 Themes: Choose from 5 free themes or buy 30 premium ones in the Mint Shop.

🪙 Economy: Complete habits → Earn Coins & XP → Level Up → Unlock Avatar Stages.
