Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is this app?

This app helps you build positive habits and track your daily progress.

2. Is the app free?

Yes, the basic features are free to use. Additional premium features may be introduced in the future.

3. How do I create a habit?

Tap the "Add Habit" button, enter your habit details, and choose a schedule.

4. How are streaks calculated?

A streak increases each time you complete a habit on its scheduled day.

5. Can I edit or delete a habit?

Yes. Open the habit and select Edit or Delete.

6. Will I receive reminders?

Yes, if notifications are enabled on your device.

7. Is my data secure?

We use reasonable security measures to protect your data.

8. Can I use the app offline?

Yes. Most habit tracking features work without an internet connection.

9. How do I delete my data?

Contact SK Technologies support or use the account deletion option if available.
